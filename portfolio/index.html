About Me & My Work

I’m Abhishek Gupta, A student of B.tech CS 2nd year student & A passionate photographer and visual storyteller, Vlogger, capturing moments where technology, nature, and human emotion intersect.

My journey in photography began with curiosity — observing light, shadows, and storytelling through images. Through sky, street, nature, architecture, and experimental photography, I aim to freeze moments that feel alive, meaningful, and futuristic.

Each image reflects my passion for visual storytelling and technical excellence.
